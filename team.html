The Team

Lewis - Owner & Personal Trainer

With 6 years of experience in the fitness industry, Lewis has successfully guided many individuals through tailored training sessions. Specialising in fat loss and muscle building, his coaching philosophy is rooted in a holistic approach, empowering clients to make lasting, healthy lifestyle changes while ensuring they enjoy the journey to sustainable results.

Ollie - Sports Massage Therapist

Ollie has a comprehensive background in sports education, having completed Level 3, Level 4, and Level 5 sports courses at CRC. In addition, he has attained both Level 3 and Level 4 certifications in sports massage therapy.

Ollie is passionate about sports massage because it allows him to combine his expertise with a genuine desire to help others. He believes assisting individuals in their recovery and supporting them in achieving their fitness and sporting goals is truly fulfilling, and it is a privilege to contribute to their journey.
